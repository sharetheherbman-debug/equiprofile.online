# EquiProfile — Product Overview

## What is EquiProfile?

EquiProfile (equiprofile.online) is a professional horse management platform for individual horse owners, trainers, and stables.  
It lets you track health records, training sessions, competitions, feeding, documents, AI-powered weather analysis, and more — all in one place.

## Target Users

- Individual horse owners (1–5 horses)
- Professional riders and trainers
- Stable managers (up to 20 horses, team members)

## Core Value Proposition

- Single platform for all equine records — no more spreadsheets or paper files
- AI weather analysis tells you whether today is safe to ride
- Role-based team access for stable staff
- Export data any time to CSV/PDF — you own your data
- 7-day free trial, no credit card needed

## Key Features

- Horse profiles & health records (vaccinations, dental, hoof care, treatments)
- Training session logging & competition tracking
- AI weather analysis with riding suitability score
- Document storage (X-rays, passports, vet reports)
- Pedigree & breeding management
- Stable team management with role-based permissions
- Nutrition & feeding cost tracking
- Lesson scheduling & client portal
- Reports & analytics dashboard
- Full data export (CSV / PDF)
