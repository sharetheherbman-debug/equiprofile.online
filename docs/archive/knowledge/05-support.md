## Common Questions

### Billing & Subscription

- **How do I upgrade?** Go to Settings → Billing, or visit /pricing and click Subscribe.
- **How do I cancel?** Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the billing period ends.
- **How do I download invoices?** Settings → Billing → Billing Portal (Stripe Portal opens with your invoice history).
- **My payment failed** — update your card in Settings → Billing → Billing Portal.
- **I was charged twice** — contact support immediately at hello@equiprofile.online; include your email address.
- **Subscription not reflecting** — log out and log back in; if the issue persists contact support.

### Technical Issues

- **Page not loading** — try a hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R or Cmd+Shift+R); clear browser cache; try another browser.
- **Slow performance** — EquiProfile works best on modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). Ensure you are on a stable internet connection.
- **Data not saving** — check your subscription status; trial-expired accounts are read-only.
- **Mobile issues** — EquiProfile is fully responsive. If something looks wrong on mobile, try rotating your device or zooming out.

### Account Issues

- **Cannot log in** — use Forgot Password on the login page.
- **Account locked** — wait 15 minutes after too many failed login attempts.
- **Email not verified** — check spam; resend verification from the login page.
- **Name or email change** — go to Settings → Profile.
- **Delete account** — email hello@equiprofile.online; data removed within 30 days.

### Data & Privacy

- All data is stored on secure UK servers.
- EquiProfile does not sell your data to third parties.
- You can export all your data to CSV/PDF at any time (paid plans).
- Full privacy policy at https://equiprofile.online/privacy.
