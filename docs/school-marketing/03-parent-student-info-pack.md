# Parent & Student Information Pack

## Welcome to EquiProfile

EquiProfile is a professional equine learning platform used by your riding school to deliver and track equestrian education. This guide explains what your child (or you, as a student) will experience.

---

## What Is EquiProfile?

EquiProfile gives every student their own **personal equestrian dashboard** — the same professional tools used by working yards, adapted for learners. It's where students manage horses, study equine theory, complete assignments, and build a portfolio of their knowledge.

## What Students Can Do

### 🐴 Horse Management
- Manage a virtual or assigned real horse
- Log daily care tasks (feeding, grooming, health checks)
- Develop professional routines and instincts

### 📚 Structured Learning
- Follow **12 professional pathways** covering horse care, riding, safety, behaviour, health, and more
- Each lesson includes clear objectives, knowledge checks, and practical applications
- Content aligned with BHS and Pony Club syllabi

### 🤖 AI Tutor
- Ask questions about equine care anytime
- Get clear, knowledgeable answers instantly
- Available 24/7 to support learning outside lesson time

### 📝 Assignments
- Receive work from their teacher
- Upload PDF submissions
- Get marks and detailed feedback
- Track assignment history

### 📊 Progress Tracking
- See progress across all skill areas
- Competency maps showing strengths and areas to improve
- Achievement badges for milestones
- Verifiable portfolio for assessments and qualifications

### 💬 Teacher Communication
- Receive messages from their instructor
- Access shared teaching resources (PDFs, images, documents)
- Get personalised lesson reviews and feedback

## How It Works

1. Your school sends an **invite link** to the student's email
2. The student creates an account (free to set up)
3. They're automatically connected to the school and their teacher
4. Everything happens through the EquiProfile student dashboard

## Privacy & Safety

- Student data is stored securely and encrypted
- Students can only see their own information
- Teachers can only see students assigned to them
- No social features, public profiles, or external sharing
- GDPR compliant

## Cost

The school manages the subscription — there is **no additional cost to students** under school plans. Individual students not enrolled through a school can subscribe from £8/month.
