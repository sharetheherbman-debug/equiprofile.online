# Teacher Onboarding Guide

Welcome to EquiProfile! This guide helps you get started as a teacher on the platform. You'll be managing students, delivering lessons, reviewing work, and tracking progress.

---

## Getting Started

### Accepting Your Invitation
1. You'll receive an email invitation from your school
2. Click the link to create your EquiProfile account
3. You're automatically connected to your school and assigned students

### Your Teacher Dashboard
When you log in, you'll see your **Teacher Dashboard** with these sections:

| Section | What It Does |
|---------|-------------|
| **Home** | Overview of your students, tasks, and activity |
| **Students** | View all assigned students and their progress |
| **Groups** | Create and manage classes/cohorts |
| **Assign Work** | Create tasks and assignments for students |
| **Lessons** | Browse and assign lessons from 12 pathways |
| **Resources** | Upload and share teaching materials |
| **Messages** | Communicate directly with students |
| **Reviews** | Provide feedback on student work and progress |
| **Progress** | Track student development across competencies |
| **Reports** | Generate and send detailed progress reports |

---

## Key Features

### Managing Students
- View all students assigned to you
- See individual student summaries (progress, competencies, activity)
- Give feedback directly from the student view

### Creating Groups
- Organise students by class, level, or cohort
- Share resources with specific groups
- Track group progress together

### Assigning Work
- Create tasks with descriptions and due dates
- Students see assigned work in their dashboard
- Track completion status

### Lesson Management
- Browse 12 professional pathways with dozens of lessons
- Assign specific lessons to students
- Review student lesson completion and quiz scores
- Write lesson reviews with recommendations

### Uploading Resources
- Upload PDFs, images, and documents
- Share with all students, specific groups, or individuals
- Students access resources from their dashboard

### Messaging
- Send messages to individual students
- Per-student conversation threads
- Simple, focused communication

### Student Progress
- Track competency development across skill areas
- View detailed progress for each student
- Identify strengths and areas needing support

### Generating Reports
- Use pre-built report templates
- Generate detailed progress reports
- Send reports to individual students or groups
- Evidence-based reporting for parents and inspectors

---

## Best Practices

1. **Set up groups first** — Organise students before assigning work
2. **Use lesson pathways** — Leverage the built-in content before creating custom work
3. **Regular feedback** — Students benefit most from consistent, timely reviews
4. **Check progress weekly** — Identify students who are falling behind early
5. **Share resources** — Upload your own materials to complement built-in lessons
