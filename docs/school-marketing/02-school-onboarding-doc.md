# School Onboarding Guide

Welcome to EquiProfile! This guide walks you through setting up your school account and getting your teachers and students started.

---

## Step 1: Create Your School Account

1. Visit [equiprofile.online/register](https://equiprofile.online/register)
2. Select **School / Organisation** during registration
3. Choose your plan tier based on student numbers
4. Your 7-day free trial begins immediately

## Step 2: Set Up Your Organisation

1. Navigate to your **School Dashboard**
2. Click **Create Organisation**
3. Enter your school name and description
4. Your plan tier determines student and teacher limits

## Step 3: Invite Teachers

1. Go to **Members** in your school dashboard
2. Click **Invite Teacher**
3. Enter each teacher's email address
4. They'll receive an invitation link to join your school

### Teacher Capabilities:
- Manage assigned students
- Upload teaching resources (PDFs, images, documents)
- Create and assign work
- Review student submissions
- Generate progress reports
- Send messages to students

## Step 4: Invite Students

1. Go to **Members** → **Invite Student**
2. Enter student email addresses (or share invite links)
3. Students create their account and are automatically linked to your school

### Student Capabilities:
- Access structured lesson pathways
- Complete assignments and submit work
- Track progress with competency maps
- Use the AI tutor for equine questions
- Receive teacher feedback and resources

## Step 5: Organise Classes & Groups

1. Teachers can create **Groups** to organise students by class, level, or cohort
2. Assign students to groups
3. Share resources with specific groups or all students
4. Track group progress together

## Step 6: Start Teaching

1. Teachers assign lessons from the 12 built-in pathways
2. Set custom assignments with due dates
3. Students complete work and submit PDFs
4. Teachers review, mark, and provide feedback
